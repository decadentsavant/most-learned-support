Support & Help

Getting Started

When you first open Most Learned, you'll be guided through a simple onboarding process with training entries that teach you how to use the app effectively.

How It Works

  1. Create Daily Entries: Write down insights, goals, or lessons you want to remember
  2. Set Recurrence: Choose when and how often each entry should resurface
  3. Daily Review: Open the app each day to see your scheduled entries
  4. Focus Sessions: Use press-and-hold focus sessions to internalize your learnings

Managing Your Entries

  • Swipe Right: Toggle entries between active and inactive
  • Swipe Left: Delete entries (with confirmation)
  • Search: Find specific entries using the search function
  • Sorting: Entries are automatically sorted with active entries first, then by creation date

Account Management

Your account is secured with Firebase authentication. You can:

  • Change your email address (requires password verification)
  • Reset your password via email
  • Switch between light and dark themes
  • Sign out securely

Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues:

  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection
  • Try signing out and signing back in
  • Ensure your app is updated to the latest version
  • Contact support if problems persist
